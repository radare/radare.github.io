CONFERENCE

Thanks to the COVID19 global pandemic we decided to make the conference purely online. r2con is a the annual conference about radare2 The conference aims to gather all the r2 users and developers around the world to enjoy a 4 day event full of experiments, hacking, teaching, discussing and coding stuff while having fun and drinking beers (safely at home). Join the Telegram Channel (broadcast) for further updates or refresh this page.
